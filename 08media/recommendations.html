Title Genre Synopsis Episodes Year User Score
Romance The Apothecary Diaries follows Maomao, a skilled apothecary from the red-light district who is kidnapped and sold into service as a maid in a beautiful, mysterious Chinese-inspired imperial palace. Her extensive knowledge of medicine and poisons helps her to solve perplexing medical and political mysteries within the Rear Palace. She catches the eye of the head eunuch, Jinshi, who recognizes her talents and brings her into a position where she can use her deductive skills to assist in uncovering conspiracies and protecting the imperial family. 48 2023-2025 8.6/10
Dark Fantasy Attack on Titan (Japanese: 進撃の巨人, Hepburn: Shingeki no Kyojin; lit. 'The Advancing Giant') is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. Set in a world where humanity is forced to live in cities surrounded by three enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans, the story follows Eren Yeager, an adolescent boy who vows to exterminate the Titans after they bring about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother. 94 2013-2023 9.1/10
High Fantasy Black Clover follows Asta, an orphan born without magic in a world where magic is everything, who dreams of becoming the Wizard King. He receives a mysterious five-leaf clover grimoire with anti-magic powers and joins the Magic Knights to achieve his goal alongside his genius rival, Yuno. The story follows their adventures as they join different Magic Knight squads, face powerful devils and elves, and uncover conspiracies to protect the Clover Kingdom. 170 2017-2021 7.9/10
Fantasy Blue Exorcist follows 15-year-old Rin Okumura, who discovers he is the son of Satan, a demon god, and inherits demonic blue flames. After his guardian's death, Rin enrolls in the True Cross Academy to become an Exorcist, training to control his demonic powers and fight against other demons to protect the human world, Assiah, from the demon world, Gehenna. 131 2011-2024 7.5/10
Sports Blue Lock is a sports anime/manga about Japan's goal to create a world-class striker by gathering 300 young forwards in a brutal, prison-like facility to compete in a grueling survival training program. The project's visionary coach, Jinpachi Ego, believes individual "egoism" and a singular drive for self-improvement are essential to "destroy Japanese loser football" and produce a goal-scoring "super striker". The story follows protagonist Yoichi Isagi and the others as they battle for supremacy, with the winner becoming Japan's ultimate striker and the losers banned from the national team for life. 38 2022-2024 8.2/10
Dark Fantasy Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is murdered and his sister Nezuko is transformed into a demon. His mission is to find the demon who killed his family and a way to cure Nezuko. He joins the Demon Slayer Corps, an organization dedicated to hunting demons, and battles powerful foes with his comrades, seeking to defeat the Demon King, Muzan Kibutsuji. 63 2019-2025 9.5/10
